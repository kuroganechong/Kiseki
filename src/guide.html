Takes into account all ATK/CDMG/Flat ATK buffs you get from gears, skills, enchants, runes, perks, and artifacts, then calculates the optimum amount of ATK/CDMG lines you should aim for in your gears. It aims to balance between your ATK and CDMG in order to maximise damage per AUTO ATTACK hit.

Enemies are taken to be at 0 DEF (ie. test dummy), and assumes all buffs are at its maximum stacks and every buff stacks together.

The formula is:
Damage per AUTO ATTACK hit = [(Stat-menu-ATK x (100% + perk-&-skill-%ATK-buff)) + flat-ATK] x (200% + Stat-menu-CDMG + CDMG-buff) x DMG-boost%

This formula is still applicable towards enemies with non-zero DEF as the damage reduction by DEF is calculated after the above formula.

How to use this calculator?
  1. Select your hero.
  2. Fill in your hero's gear setup, and desired number of ATK/CDMG lines.
  3. Select the skill/perk buffs you want to take into account of. The stats for each skill are based on lvl90, maxed book effects stats, and are simplified to ATK, CDMG, Flat ATK. If you are unsure about the skill mechanics please refer to relevant Mask-of-Goblin page.
  4. Fill in the artifact stats.
  5. Fill in any other additional stats (guild buffs, dmg boost, ignored stats in skill selection) in the last row. Refer to this list.
  6. For the first run, fill in the observed damage in test dummy and click calibrate.
  7. Click calculate and view your results.
  8. Repeat for any other hero you want.

This calculator does not take into account the following:

  • Skill-only damage boost %
  • Skill ATK multiplier
  • Skill-only ATK/CDMG/Flat ATK buff
  • Attack speed/Penetration
  • Additional attack per hit (Kasel UW effect, Phillop HP% damage etc.)

Note: If you wish to apply this to skills, it is not entirely correct because each skill has a different scaling based on hero's ATK value and has a base value.
A proposed formula would be
Skill-damage per hit = [ Base + ((Stat-menu-ATK) x ATK%-buff + Flat-ATK) x scaling ] x Total-CDMG

It also doesn't take into account the following, however you can workaround these by adding them at the additional stats row at the bottom of the page: (you will need to calculate the amount to be added by yourself)

  • Guild buffs
  • Damage boost % (Increase damage by ..., boss damage etc.)
  • Additional ATK boost based on other character stats (eg. Mediana S3)
  • Additional ATK boost based on other stats (PDEF, Mana/sec etc.)
